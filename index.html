About Me

Software engineer with more than 12 years of experience in IT industry. More than 8 years in mobile apps development.

Curious to know how things work and interested in making things work.

Most of my work involved different aspects mobile application development for retail clients.

Started my career with Windows Mobile development in C#.

For last few years, working exclusively in iOS.

What I love most about my work?

The possibility of transforming an idea to something a human can touch and feel.

Coding an emotion. Packing an experience. Applying artistic logic. The list goes on.

Highlights

  • 2017: Developed a functional prototype and led the team to develop an iOS audiobook application for an US based beauty products manufacturer.
  • 2016: Architected and developed a robotic arm as a proof of concept for an US based department stores chain. The idea was to achieve hundred percent test coverage in automated testing suite.
  • 2015: Architected and developed the first customer facing price check application for the same client, exclusively developed for the Puerto Rico store.
  • 2012: Successfully led the team to develop the first version of customer facing retail iOS application for the largest beauty retailer in US.
  • 2011: Successfully led the team to develop the first version of customer facing iOS application for world's largest broad line food distributor.
  • 2009: Co-founded a mobile application development start up, Techions Software Labs.
